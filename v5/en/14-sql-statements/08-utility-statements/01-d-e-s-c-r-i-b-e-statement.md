### 13.8.1 DESCRIBE Statement

The [`DESCRIBE`](describe.html "13.8.1 DESCRIBE Statement") and [`EXPLAIN`](explain.html "13.8.2 EXPLAIN Statement") statements are synonyms, used either to obtain information about table structure or query execution plans. For more information, see [Section 13.7.5.5, “SHOW COLUMNS Statement”](show-columns.html "13.7.5.5 SHOW COLUMNS Statement"), and [Section 13.8.2, “EXPLAIN Statement”](explain.html "13.8.2 EXPLAIN Statement").
