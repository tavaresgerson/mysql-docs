#### 19.5.1.15 Replication and Fractional Seconds Support

MySQL 8.0 permits fractional seconds for
[`TIME`](time.html "13.2.3 The TIME Type"),
[`DATETIME`](datetime.html "13.2.2 The DATE, DATETIME, and TIMESTAMP Types"), and
[`TIMESTAMP`](datetime.html "13.2.2 The DATE, DATETIME, and TIMESTAMP Types") values, with up to
microseconds (6 digits) precision. See
[Section 13.2.6, “Fractional Seconds in Time Values”](fractional-seconds.html "13.2.6 Fractional Seconds in Time Values").
